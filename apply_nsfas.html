NSFAS Application

Apply for NSFAS funding easily through Top Student. We’ll help you upload what’s needed. We’ll contact you if there are issues or missing documents.

Required Documents

  • Copy of the applicant’s ID or birth certificate
  • Copy of parent(s)/guardian(s) ID
  • Proof of income (payslip, affidavit, etc.)
  • Latest academic results
  • Proof of address (e.g. municipal bill)
