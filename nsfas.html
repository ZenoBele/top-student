What is NSFAS?

NSFAS (National Student Financial Aid Scheme) is a government-funded program that provides financial assistance to eligible South African students studying at public universities or TVET colleges.

How Top Student Applies for You

Top Student simplifies your NSFAS application. We help by filling out forms accurately, checking required documents, and submitting on your behalf, ensuring maximum chances of approval.

Reason for a Bursary

Bursaries exist to support students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds so they can access higher education and improve career prospects without worrying about tuition costs.

Minimum Requirements

  • South African citizen or permanent resident.
  • Accepted at a public university or TVET college.
  • Household income must meet NSFAS criteria.
  • Provide certified documents (ID, proof of income, academic results).

Tips for Success

  • Apply early to maximize approval chances.
  • Check all documents are certified and complete.
  • Regularly track your application status online.
  • Seek help from Top Student or NSFAS helpdesk if unsure.

Required Documents

  • Certified copy of your ID
  • Proof of household income
  • Latest academic results
  • Additional documents requested by NSFAS

Visit NSFAS.org.za for official document requirements.

Deadlines & Support

Apply before the closing date. For questions or assistance, contact NSFAS helpdesk or Top Student support team.
