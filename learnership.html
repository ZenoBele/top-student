Kickstart Your Career with a Learnership

Learnerships are structured programs that blend theory with workplace practice — helping you earn, learn, and grow your career.

Explore Opportunities

What is a Learnership?

A learnership is a work-based education program that combines practical experience with structured learning. It gives you a recognized qualification while gaining hands-on exposure in your field.

How It Works

  • Register with an accredited training provider or company.
  • Attend theoretical classes (online or in-person).
  • Complete structured workplace training with mentorship.
  • Obtain a qualification upon successful completion.

Why Choose a Learnership?

  • Gain a nationally recognized qualification.
  • Earn while you learn.
  • Hands-on workplace experience.
  • Boost your CV and job readiness.

How to Apply

  1. Search for accredited providers or SETA opportunities.
  2. Check entry requirements (age, qualifications, etc.).
  3. Prepare your CV and certified documents.
  4. Submit applications before deadlines.
  5. Attend interviews or assessments.
