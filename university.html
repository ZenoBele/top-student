Explore South African Universities

University of South Africa (UNISA)

The largest open distance learning university in Africa, serving over 400,000 students. Study at your own pace while working.

Admissions

Apply online twice a year. Ensure you meet minimum requirements and submit certified documents.

  • Applications usually open in September
  • Apply at www.unisa.ac.za
  • NSFAS funding available for qualifying students

Faculties & Colleges

  • College of Science, Engineering & Technology
  • College of Human Sciences
  • College of Education
  • College of Law
  • College of Economic & Management Sciences
  • College of Accounting Sciences
  • College of Graduate Studies

Fees & Funding

  • Average module cost: ZAR 1,800 – 2,500
  • Registration fees required before enrolment
  • NSFAS covers full costs for eligible students

Why Choose UNISA?

  • Flexible distance and online learning
  • Largest selection of courses in Africa
  • Affordable and accessible
  • Internationally recognized
Visit UNISA Official Website

University of Johannesburg (UJ)

Modern, dynamic university with over 50,000 students. Offers a wide range of programs across multiple campuses.

Admissions

Apply online for undergraduate or postgraduate programs. Ensure all academic transcripts and documents are ready.

  • Applications open around March for the following year
  • Apply at www.uj.ac.za
  • NSFAS funding available for eligible students

Faculties & Colleges

  • Faculty of Engineering & the Built Environment
  • Faculty of Health Sciences
  • Faculty of Science
  • Faculty of Humanities
  • Faculty of Law
  • Faculty of Education
  • Faculty of Economic & Financial Sciences

Fees & Funding

  • Tuition depends on program and modules selected
  • Registration fees required for official enrolment
  • NSFAS covers full costs for eligible South African students

Why Choose UJ?

  • Modern, multi-campus university
  • Wide range of programs and disciplines
  • Strong industry links and research opportunities
  • Vibrant campus life and diversity
Visit UJ Official Website

University of Cape Town (UCT)

South Africa's oldest university, renowned for academic excellence with over 30,000 students.

Admissions

Apply online for undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Ensure you meet minimum requirements and submit certified documents.

  • Applications usually open in Mid-year
  • Apply at www.uct.ac.za
  • Scholarships and financial aid available

Faculties & Colleges

  • Commerce
  • Engineering & the Built Environment
  • Health Sciences
  • Humanities
  • Law
  • Science

Fees & Funding

  • Undergraduate fees: ~ZAR 60,000 – 70,000 per year
  • Postgraduate fees vary by program
  • Scholarships and NSFAS available for eligible students

Why Choose UCT?

  • World-class academic programs
  • Beautiful campus at the foot of Table Mountain
  • Strong research focus
  • Internationally recognized degrees
Visit UCT Official Website

University of Venda (Univen)

A vibrant university located in Limpopo, offering a range of programs with strong community engagement and cultural heritage.

Admissions

Apply online for undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Ensure you meet entry requirements.

  • Applications open in September for the following year
  • Apply at www.univen.ac.za
  • NSFAS funding available for qualifying students

Faculties & Colleges

  • Faculty of Science, Engineering & Agriculture
  • Faculty of Health Sciences
  • Faculty of Humanities, Social Sciences & Education
  • Faculty of Management, Commerce & Law

Fees & Funding

  • Average annual fees: ZAR 15,000 – 25,000 depending on program
  • Registration fees required for official enrollment
  • NSFAS covers full costs for eligible students

Why Choose Univen?

  • Strong cultural and community focus
  • Modern campus facilities in a serene environment
  • One of the Top University in South Africa
  • Accessible and welcoming for all students
Visit Univen Official Website

Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT)

Located in Cape Town, CPUT is known for practical, technology-driven programs and its marine & ocean studies. Offers 30,000+ students hands-on learning.

Admissions

Applications open once a year. Ensure you meet program requirements and submit certified documents online.

  • Applications open in September
  • Apply at www.cput.ac.za
  • NSFAS funding available for qualifying students

Faculties & Programs

  • Engineering & the Built Environment
  • Business & Management Sciences
  • Education & Social Sciences
  • Applied Sciences, including Marine & Ocean Studies
  • Health & Wellness programs
  • Design, Technology & Innovation

Fees & Funding

  • Average annual tuition: ZAR 20,000 – 30,000 depending on program
  • Registration fees required before enrolment
  • NSFAS covers full costs for eligible students

Why Choose CPUT?

  • Strong technology & applied science programs
  • Hands-on practical learning experience
  • Marine & ocean-focused research programs
  • Beautiful campuses in Cape Town
Visit CPUT Official Website
