About Top Student

Top Student was founded by Vhulenda Bele, a South African student who experienced first-hand the challenges learners face when preparing for exams without organised resources, guidance, and support.

The platform was created with a clear mission: to make exam preparation simple, structured, and accessible to every learner — helping students stay organised, confident, and fully prepared to succeed academically.

Today, Top Student continues to focus on empowering learners by providing essential study tools, past papers, and academic support in one place.

Founder & Developer: Vhulenda Bele

Why We Built Top Student

Many students struggle because they lack access to organised study resources and guidance. Top Student was created to solve this problem — giving learners the tools, reminders, and support they need to succeed.
