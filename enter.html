Entrepreneurship is about solving problems, creating value, and turning your ideas into reality. This guide will give you the knowledge and tools to start your journey.

Start Learning

What is Entrepreneurship?

Entrepreneurship is the process of creating and running a business, innovating solutions, and taking calculated risks. It’s the foundation of economic growth and independence.

Why It Matters

  • Drives innovation and creativity
  • Creates jobs and economic growth
  • Promotes independence and leadership
  • Empowers communities

Steps to Start a Business

  1. Identify a problem or opportunity
  2. Develop a solution or product idea
  3. Create a business plan
  4. Secure funding or resources
  5. Launch and market your business

Key Entrepreneurial Skills

  • Problem-solving & critical thinking
  • Leadership & teamwork
  • Financial literacy
  • Resilience & adaptability
  • Networking & communication
